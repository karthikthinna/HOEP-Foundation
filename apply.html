Who Qualifies for Support

We serve motivated individuals who have a clear path to self-sufficiency but face a specific financial or systemic barrier holding them back.

  • You are from an underserved or low-income background
  • You have a specific, identified need (education, training, or medical)
  • You have a clear plan for how support will change your situation
  • You are committed to self-sufficiency, not ongoing dependence
  • Children, single women, men, and veterans are all welcome to apply
